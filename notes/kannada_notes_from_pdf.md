# 🟦 Page 2 – Sentence Structure

## ❓ Question Words

| Kannada | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| enu | what |
| ninna | your |
| hesaru | name |

Correct:  
ninna hesaru enu  

Incorrect:  
enu nimma hesaru  

---

## 🧠 Key Rule

- Question word comes at the end

---

## 🔁 Sentence Structure Difference

| English | Kannada |
|--------|--------|
| Subject – Verb – Object | Subject – Object – Verb |

---

## 🧾 Examples

| English | Kannada |
|--------|--------|
| Boy ate cake | huduga cake thindanu |
| Students played cricket | vidyarthigalu cricket aadidaru |
| Padma is watching TV | Padma TV nodthiddale |
| Amit ate apple | Amit apple thindanu |
| Raghav watches movies | Raghav movies nodtane |

---

## ⚠️ Note

- Kannada often does not explicitly use "is/are"

# 🟦 Page 3 – First Person Pronouns

## 👤 Pronouns

| Kannada | English |
|--------|--------|
| naanu | I |
| naavu | We |

---

## 🔗 Structure

Pronoun → Object → Verb Ending

---

## 🔧 Verb Endings

| Pronoun | Ending | Meaning |
|--------|--------|--------|
| naanu | iddeeni | I am |
| naavu | iddevi | We are |

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu office alli iddeeni → I am in office  
- naavu Delhi alli iddevi → We are in Delhi  
- naavu Karnataka dalli iddevi → We are in Karnataka  

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- alli → in  
- Verb endings change based on context/respect

---

# 🟦 Page 10 – Interrogatives

## ❓ Question Words

| Kannada | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| yaaru | who |
| enu | what |
| hege | how |
| yaaradhu | whose |
| yaava | which |
| yaarige | whom |
| yaavaga | when |
| yelli | where |
| yake | why |
| yeshtu | how much |
| yeshtu gante | what time |

---

## 🧠 Rules

- Usually at end of sentence  
- Can appear at start in casual speech  

---

## 🧾 Examples

- avanu hege iddane → How is he?  
- ee pustaka yaaradhu → Whose book is this?  
- neevu yaavaga barteeri → When will you come?  

---

# 🟦 Page 11 – Demonstrative Pronouns

## 🧠 Concept
- Used to indicate **near / far objects**
- Help in **identification**

---

## 📌 Forms

| Type | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Near | idu (this) | ivu (these) |
| Far | adu (that) | avu (those) |

> For people:
- ivaru → these people  
- avaru → those people  

---

## 🧾 Examples

| Kannada | English |
|--------|--------|
| idu nanna naayi | This is my dog |
| ivanu Rahul | This is Rahul |
| ivaru olle janaru | These are good people |
| ivu nanna pustakagalu | These are my books |
| adu nanna bekku | That is my cat |
| avaru olle janaru | Those are good people |

---

# 🟦 Page 13 – Conversations

## 1️⃣ Hello

A: namaskara  
B: namaskara  
A: neevu yaaru?  
B: naanu James  
A: neevu American aa?  
B: howdu, naanu American  

---

## 2️⃣ Nationality

A: avaru yaaru?  
B: avaru vidyarthi (He is a student)  

A: avanu Bharatiya na?  
B: illa, avanu British  

A: avalu yaaru?  
B: avalu Chinese  

A: avanu vidyarthi na?  
B: howdu  

---

## 3️⃣ What is your name?

A: nimma hesaru enu?  
B: nanna hesaru Sam  

A: neevu teacher aa?  
B: illa, naanu shikshaka  

A: avalu hesaru enu?  
B: avalu hesaru Anita
