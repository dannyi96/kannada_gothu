# 🟦 Page 2 – Sentence Structure

## ❓ Question Words

| Kannada | Meaning |
|--------|--------|

| ninna | your |
| hesaru | name |

Correct:  
ninna hesaru enu  

Incorrect:  
enu nimma hesaru  

---

## 🧠 Key Rule

- Question word comes at the end

---

## 🔁 Sentence Structure Difference

| English | Kannada |
|--------|--------|
| Subject – Verb – Object | Subject – Object – Verb |

---

## 🧾 Examples

| English | Kannada |
|--------|--------|
| Boy ate cake | huduga cake thindanu |
| Students played cricket | vidyarthigalu cricket aadidaru |
| Padma is watching TV | Padma TV nodthiddale |
| Amit ate apple | Amit apple thindanu |
| Raghav watches movies | Raghav movies nodtane |

---

## ⚠️ Note

- Kannada often does not explicitly use "is/are"

# 🟦 Page 3 – First Person Pronouns

## 👤 Pronouns

| Kannada | English |
|--------|--------|
| naanu | I |
| naavu | We |

---

## 🔗 Structure

Pronoun → Object → Verb Ending

---

## 🔧 Verb Endings

| Pronoun | Ending | Meaning |
|--------|--------|--------|
| naanu | iddeeni | I am |
| naavu | iddevi | We are |

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu office alli iddeeni → I am in office  
- naavu Delhi alli iddevi → We are in Delhi  
- naavu Karnataka dalli iddevi → We are in Karnataka  

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- alli → in  
- Verb endings change based on context/respect

# 🟦 Page 11 – Demonstrative Pronouns

## 🧠 Concept
- Used to indicate **near / far objects**
- Help in **identification**

---

## 📌 Forms

| Type | Singular | Plural |
|------|----------|--------|
| Near | idu (this) | ivu (these) |
| Far | adu (that) | avu (those) |

> For people:
- ivaru → these people  
- avaru → those people  

---

## 🧾 Examples

| Kannada | English |
|--------|--------|
| idu nanna naayi | This is my dog |
| ivanu Rahul | This is Rahul |
| ivaru olle janaru | These are good people |
| ivu nanna pustakagalu | These are my books |
| adu nanna bekku | That is my cat |
| avaru olle janaru | Those are good people |

# 🟦 Page 31 – Location Words (alli, illi, alli vs illi)

## 🧠 Key Words

| Kannada | Meaning |
|--------|--------|
| illi | here |
| alli | there |
| elli | where |

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu illi iddeeni → I am here  
- avanu alli idaane → He is there  
- neevu elli ideeri → Where are you?  

---

## 🧠 Pattern

- illi = near speaker  
- alli = away from speaker  
- elli = question  

---

# 🟦 Page 32 – “in / at” Usage (alli)

## 🧠 Rule

- **alli = in / at**

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu office alli iddeeni → I am in office  
- avanu mane alli idaane → He is at home  
- avaru Bengaluru alli idaare → They are in Bengaluru  

---

## ⚠️ Note

- Kannada uses **postpositions**, not prepositions  

---

# 🟦 Page 33 – Negative Forms (illa)

## 🧠 Key Word

- **illa = not / does not exist**

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu busy illa → I am not busy

- meeting illa → There is no meeting  
- neeru illa → No water  

---

## 🧠 Pattern

- noun + illa → absence  

---

# 🟦 Page 34 – “ide” vs “illa”

## 🧠 Concept

## 🧾 Examples

---

## 🧠 Usage

# 🟦 Page 40 – Present Continuous (Basic Form)

## 🧠 Pattern

**Verb root + th + person ending**

---

## 🧾 Examples

- naanu maadthiddini → I am doing  
- naanu hogthiddini → I am going  
- naanu barthiddini → I am coming  

---
